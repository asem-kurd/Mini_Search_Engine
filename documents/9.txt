
Data science is an interdisciplinary field that combines expertise in statistics, computer science, and domain knowledge to analyze large volumes of data. Its goal is to extract meaningful insights that can guide decision-making in various industries. From predicting consumer behavior in marketing to optimizing supply chains in logistics, data science has become a cornerstone of modern business operations. The field heavily relies on tools like Python, R, and SQL, and employs methods such as data cleaning, machine learning, and visualization to make sense of both structured and unstructured data.
